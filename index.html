Frozen Mint is a refreshing, clean-label cold drink crafted for people who want both invigorating taste and mindful nutrition in every sip.

What truly defines Thanda as a brand is its health-first philosophy:

  • Zero added sugar – making it suitable for calorie-conscious consumers and those avoiding refined sugars
  • Light and hydrating – perfect for hot days or post-activity refreshment
  • Naturally inspired flavoring – designed to feel closer to real ingredients rather than artificial sweetness
  • Guilt-free indulgence – a drink you can enjoy regularly without compromising your lifestyle goals
